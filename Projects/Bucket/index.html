Dating Site for Janitors

A completed website final project for CGT141. This dating website helps janitors, across the globe, find love based on the location that they maintain.

Description:

According to studies provided by Bloomberg.com, Janitors and other building cleaners tend to marry individuals in the same occupation. However, their tedious work and long hours often leave them with no time to find love. That's where Bucket comes in. This website/mobile application pairs janitorial staff based on the building that they maintain. Simply sweep™ right on those you find appealing, and find love today!
