HosDB

All-in-one Hospital Patient Database.

After conducting many, different ethnographic interviews regarding patient-care at small hospitals, my TECH120 group and I created a solution for easy, efficient patient management. Programmed using Microsoft Visual Basic.
Last update: December 2nd, 2019

Overview

Team Members:

Jared Grounds, Michael Rosswurm, and Adnane Sentoussi

Global Challenge:

Health Informatics

Problem Statement:

Health professionals, practitioners, patients and local governments need a secure, online patient-database because current hospital databases can be unprotected at times, and are often difficult for medical professionals to use.

Our solution:

An all-in-one patient database. This allows practitioners to easily enter in basic patient info, additional notes, and their billing statement. Hospital administrators can also view any changes made to patient files, migrate files from one employee to another, and much more.
